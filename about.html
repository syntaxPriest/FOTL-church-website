OUR WAYS OF OPERATION

The way and manners in which we are operating are as follows:

1. We would be having a Fellowship Center as a base in each town or state

2. We would be having a very big and extended Secretariat in each state or town, whereby we will be able to operate through all our Department Ministries.

3. We would be having state coordinators, national coordinators or ministers that will be able to take care of all the Departmental ministries in his/her state/nation successful with the help of Holy Spirit.

VISION, MISSION, MOTTO


The Finger of the Lord Ministries International was born on 30th of July, 1985, after the Baptism of the Holy Ghost, (which we have been long search for).

Immediately the Holy Ghost came, the eight years Ministry totally changed to powerful Ministrations- starting from my then- station (under Gospel Faith Mission) Okejebu -Idanre, in Akure District, Ondo state, on 31st July, 1985, in which Thirteen out of the fifteen attendants of that Wednesday Gospel service night was baptized with the Holy Spirit.

After this first attempt, the ministrations continued with the people individually before, during and after Convention that was held in Ibadan, between 3rd and 11th August, 1985. The fame of which went abroad cause trooped invitations from different churches, that draws my Ministrations far and wide such as GOSPEL FAITH MISSION at Owena, September, 1985, at Agege Lagos, October 1985, at Agbado, Lagos, September 12th to 21st, 1985, at Owo, October 1985, RCCG, Ile-Oluji and Elim Evangelical Church, Ile-Oluji, etc..


Our Vision


To provide sound Christian teaching for all members and adherents who practice holiness and seek to be baptized in the Holy Spirit. (Hebrews 12: 14, Acts 1:8)

Our mission


To preach the Gospel with signs following and establish self – propagating churches, which believe and obey the great commission. (Mathew 28: 16 – 20).

Our motto


Serving the Nations with the Word.

Our Core Values

We are committed to nurturing in our Assemblies, ministries, organs and programs the following values:

Ministry

We are committed to living, preaching and teaching the word of God. Our love for God and His calling shall be our driving force. The power and influence of the Holy Spirit is the key agent of transformation. We value, appreciate and recognize the diversity of gifts in the Body of Christ.

Leadership

We are committed to a Godly transformation leadership in church and society that equips believers so that they can fully participate in the Great Commission. People are our great assets within and without the church.

Community

We exist and work in the community. We believe that it is our responsibility to impact them in a holistic manner. We stand in solidarity with those who are vulnerable in society.

Integrity

We are committed to personal integrity and Holiness. We are committed to teaching and practicing sound biblical doctrine.

Integrity

We are committed to a Godly transformation leadership in church and society that equips believers so that they can fully participate in the Great Commission. People are our great assets within and without the church.

Accountability

We are committed to a culture of transparency and accountability with our members and general public.

Excellence

In keeping with our identity and ministry target, we are committed to the culture of excellence in all our service delivery and ministries.

Family

We are committed to building strong and effective families that serve the purpose that God ordained for them. We believe that the family contains of a union between a man and a woman and their offspring’s.

The sanctity of life

We uphold and respect the sanctity of life from conception to death.

Partnership

We are committed to partnering with like – minded ministries, churches para – church organizations and other agencies that subscribe to our philosophy of ministry.

OUR COVENANT AND PATTERN OF LIVING

With this vision, God entered into covenant with us (Genesis 9:12) and the token of the covenant is the First Finger and Tongue (Luke 11:20, Exodus 4:12). This covenant goes along with three conditions: Love, Holiness and Humbleness.

1. Love one another with brotherly Love, and in sharing things together. (Hebrew 13:1, 1Cor 13, John 13: 34- 35)

2. Holiness unto God (Exodus 39:30) both in Spirit, Soul and Body. (Lev. 19:2, 1Peter 1: 15-16)

3. Humbleness unto God and to everybody (1Peter 5:5-6)

Then God gave us seven stairs and pattern for us in our ministerial duties. The pattern, in which following it will lead us to successful Christian living, and well balance and living our lives diligently after having faith in God.

1. Exercising our faith to develop virtue (goodness, excellence, christian energy).

2. Exercising virtue to develop knowledge (Intelligence).

3. Exercising Knowledge to develop Temperance (Self Control)

4. Exercising self-control to develop steadfastness (Patience, Endurance)

5. Exercising steadfastness to develop Godliness (Piety).

6. Exercising Godliness to develop brotherly affection.

7. And in exercising brotherly affection to develop Christian love.

These are the seven stairs that God gave us. Seven stairs to successful life, for these are our qualities, that will keep us from being idle or unfruitful: and that will help us to make sure our calling. (2Peter 1:5-10 Amp).
