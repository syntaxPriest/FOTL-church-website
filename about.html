VISION, MISSION, MOTTO


Finger of the lord ministries international is a self-supporting and a self a propagating church established way back in the 1920s with its head office in Nyang’ori – Vihiga County:along Kisumu /Kakamega Road. This is approximately 10 Kilometers from Kisumu town.


The church has approximately 6,000 assemblies spread throughout the country with over 6000 pastors who minister in the assemblies. Our presence is in all the 47 Counties of the Republic of Kenya.

Our Vision


To provide sound Christian teaching for all members and adherents who practice holiness and seek to be baptized in the Holy Spirit. (Hebrews 12: 14, Acts 1:8)

Our mission


To preach the Gospel with signs following and establish self – propagating churches, which believe and obey the great commission. (Mathew 28: 16 – 20).

Our motto


Serving the Nations with the Word.

Our Core Values

We are committed to nurturing in our Assemblies, ministries, organs and programs the following values:

Ministry

We are committed to living, preaching and teaching the word of God. Our love for God and His calling shall be our driving force. The power and influence of the Holy Spirit is the key agent of transformation. We value, appreciate and recognize the diversity of gifts in the Body of Christ.

Leadership

We are committed to a Godly transformation leadership in church and society that equips believers so that they can fully participate in the Great Commission. People are our great assets within and without the church.

Community

We exist and work in the community. We believe that it is our responsibility to impact them in a holistic manner. We stand in solidarity with those who are vulnerable in society.

Integrity

We are committed to personal integrity and Holiness. We are committed to teaching and practicing sound biblical doctrine.

Integrity

We are committed to a Godly transformation leadership in church and society that equips believers so that they can fully participate in the Great Commission. People are our great assets within and without the church.

Accountability

We are committed to a culture of transparency and accountability with our members and general public.

Excellence

In keeping with our identity and ministry target, we are committed to the culture of excellence in all our service delivery and ministries.

Family

We are committed to building strong and effective families that serve the purpose that God ordained for them. We believe that the family contains of a union between a man and a woman and their offspring’s.

The sanctity of life

We uphold and respect the sanctity of life from conception to death.

Partnership

We are committed to partnering with like – minded ministries, churches para – church organizations and other agencies that subscribe to our philosophy of ministry.

THE PAG STATEMENT OF FAITH

This statement of fundamental truths is intended as a basis of fellowship. This statement does not claim to hold all the available truth but the irreducible minimum of all gospel ministries.


We believe that the scriptures both old and new testament, are verbally inspired of God and are the infallible revelation of God to man, serving as our own sufficient rule for faith and practice.


We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons, Father, Son and Holy Spirit. We believe that Jesus Christ was sent of God, begotten of the Holy Spirit, born of the Virgin Mary, and came into the world as savior for man’s redemption.


In the coming of Christ to the earth, in the incarnation, Gods life was housed in a body developed in Mary. He was tested by the devil in every aspect of the human life, crucified, resurrected ascended to the father, and was glorified as the incorruptible seed, the Word God (1 Peter 1: 23). This seed of life is what is planted into the dead spirits of men by the miracle of the new Birth.


Man was created in the image of God and was alienated from God by Adam’s sin. The Lord Jesus Christ came into the world, died for our sins, according to the scripture, giving his life as a sacrifice, and shedding his blood. Being reconciled by his death, the saving life of Christ becomes the new nature that is to be fed and developed by the Holy Spirit in each Christian (Romans 5: 10)


The ordinance of baptism by immersion is commanded in the scriptures for all believers, appropriating their death with Christ on Calvary and rising with him in newness of life.


Christ arose from the dead with a glorified body, ascended into heaven and is on the right hand side of the Father, as our advocate and our High Priest. In the divine planning there are three appearances of Christ: one, when he came into the world through the miracle of incarnation; two, when he returns for those who have received the cleansing of his blood (symbolizing the impartation of His life), and have made themselves ready; and three, when he returns, His Saints to reign for 1000 years.


We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit as in Acts 2: 4 . We believe that in Gods redemption plan for man, He has provided through His Son and by the Holy Spirit the miracle for healing for the body, soul and spirit.


We believe that there will be a final Judgment in which whosoever is found not written in the book of life will be consigned to the lake of fire.


We believe that God is bringing His Church to completion and expect to see an increasing demonstration of Gods supernatural grace and power in meeting human need.

CHURCH PROFILE SUMMARY

When did the church began?

The Church began in the 1920s

Which is the mother church?

Its mother church is the Pentecostal Assemblies of Canada (PAOC).

Is the church registered?

Currently, the church is registered under the Registration of Societies Act and its registration number is 1248

Why Would a Successful Entrepreneur Hire a Coach?

The church has approximately 6,000 churches with 6,000 pastors. Over two thousand pastors have been trained and the rest are undergoing training

Does it have an organized church structure?

We have an organized church structure. The head is the General Superintendent assisted by the General Secretary and the General Treasurer.

Is the church sub divided into regional blocks?

The church is sub divided into regional blocks called PAG Districts for efficient administration. Each District is made up of a minimum of twenty churches. The heads of these districts are the District Overseers.

How are the activities of the church carried?

All activities of the church, including financial transactions, are laid down in the PAG – K Constitution (1998)
